Section 1: Overview

Originator:Minnesota Department of Transportation (MnDOT)

Title: Boundaries of Minnesota

Abstract: This is the MnDOT user-accepted version of the state boundary. It is a composite of all of the county boundaries, as accepted by MnDOT for general purpose use. This boundary represents a pre-1947 state boundary as it does not recognize the 1947 states compact signed between Wisconsin, Michigan, and Minnesota, which defined the boundary/boundaries within Lake Superior. This boundary measures approximately 84,365 square miles, and excludes the portion of Lake Superior that is legally defined as part of the state of Minnesota.

Purpose: This layer was created to represent the general boundary of Minnesota for cartographic mapping and planning-level spatial analysis. This layer does not represent legal descriptions, nor should it be used for survey-grade applications.
