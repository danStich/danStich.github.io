Quantitative Biology (BIOL 217) Course Website

This site is not intended for submission of class assignments, accessing reading materials, tracking grades, or viewing the syllabus. For all of those materials, please refer to the course blackboard site, accessible through: https://blackboard.oneonta.edu .

All data used for lecture modules and homework assignments are available here.
